More Resources to be Better Prepared

    Red Cross Types of Emergencies

    This page from the Red Cross is a great resource where you can find out more information on various disasters; including what to do to prepare for them, what to do during, and what to do after.

    Ready.gov

    Ready.gov gives detailed information on what you need to do to be prepared for an emergency. It gives information for a wide spectrum of scenarios and will help ensure you have a solid plan in place. CDC Emergency Preparedness

    The first two sites have a lot of information, so it can be a bit tough to find exactly what you need. This site has a simple user interface that gives you a detialed list for an emergency kit, helps with making a plan, and provides where you can find more information about local emergency services. Seattle Dept. of Emergency Management

    On this site you can get information specific to Seattle and its emergency plans. It also includes a list of natural disasters Seattle is at risk of, with a map showing which areas are most vulnerable.
